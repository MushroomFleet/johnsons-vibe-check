## What this is

**Johnson's Vibe-Check** is a research framework and a portable analytic **procedure**. It treats every film, show, or franchise instalment as an artifact that encodes a **model of its intended audience** — and gives you a repeatable way to reconstruct that model, diagnose where it broke, and predict whether a work will be rejected, rehabilitated, or forgotten.

The central move is **reading the shadow, not the glare.** A production's *stated* intention — press kit, interviews, marketing — is bright light that obscures the object. But the choices baked into the cut (what gets re-explained, how brightly good and evil are signposted, what's built to be sold, the cutting rhythm) cast a **shadow**: the *revealed* model of the audience, which no one defends in an interview because no one made it as a conscious claim. **Revealed preference beats stated preference** — the testimony is performance; the artifact is confession.

The framework is calibrated on a single clean case (Obi-Wan Kenobi across 1977 → 1999 → 2022) and then generalised into an instrument that can be pointed at any production.

> **The one rule of hygiene:** *We do not question them. We question the demographic they encoded.* The makers' values are never the object — their **model of the reader** is. This restriction is what keeps the instrument falsifiable rather than factional.

---

## What it's for

Use it to answer questions like:

- **Why was this rejected on arrival, then loved a decade later?** (Or: will it be?)
- **Why did a precisely-engineered tentpole flop while a scrappy no-budget film endured?**
- **What did the producers think their audience *was*, and what did they bet it would *become*?**
- **Is a "failure" actually a failure — or a priced-in move at the level of the slate?**
- **Will this franchise age out with its cohort, or transmit to the next generation?**

It is built for critics, researchers, writers, and anyone who workshops characters or franchises and wants to target a demographic **at the concept stage** — by *reflection* or *appeal* — and understand the very different risk profiles of those two paths.

## The core procedure

At its heart is a **three-question temporal sweep** — three readings at three tenses of the same encoded reader:

1. **"Who were they speaking to?"** — the *present-tense address.* Read from exposition density, register, in-jokes, what's assumed-known vs. explained.
2. **"Who do they think we are?"** — the *implied present audience* (**Shadow 1, the position error**). Read from revealed choices, not stated intent.
3. **"Who did they think we would become?"** — the *forecast audience* (**Shadow 2, the velocity error**). The bet with a settlement date.

**The diagnosis lives in the GAPS between the three answers, not the answers themselves.** When all three align, you tend to find a durable work. When they diverge, the sweep tells you *at which tense* the audience-model broke.

A mandatory gate is asked **first**:

- **Q0 — the slate gate:** *At what unit was this greenlit, and what does failure cost the slate?* The unit of intention is often the **portfolio**, not the film — and a loss can be fully priced in (rights-hold, trial balloon, sacrificial anode). A strategic-loss reading is admissible **only with a fingerprint visible before settlement**, or it becomes an unfalsifiable conspiracy theory.

And the whole thing runs as an **experimental loop**: pick a case → Q0 → run the sweep *blind* → read the gaps → record a prediction → check against a **non-gross settlement signal** → **support / challenge / refine.**

## Key findings so far

- **Rehabilitation is generational, not critical.** Works are rehabilitated not by aging into nostalgia but the moment a **worse successor** arrives to be measured against — and only if they have **authored fuel** for that spark to catch.
- **Two shadows, one root.** "Who we are" (position error) and "who we'll become" (velocity error) are the **zeroth and first derivatives of one lagged instrument** — the only tool for reading the audience is the recently departed past, so producers systematically address *who we just finished being.*
- **Durability tracks the *reality of the lock*, not the *precision of the aim*.** Established by a matched off-diagonal pair: a precisely-targeted flop (phantom lock) against a sloppily-made endurer (real lock).
- **Two ways to cut the key — appeal vs. reflection.** Engineered top-down *appeal* is phantom-prone; bottom-up *reflection* is phantom-immune but scale-risky. The felt risk is **inverted** from the true risk.
- **Data builds derivatives; only reflection builds originals** (keystone). Algorithms and playbooks are the industrialised lag — blind to any demographic that hasn't yet generated data, which is exactly where new landmarks are born. *A genuinely new cultural landmark cannot be manufactured from data, because data is the fossil record of old landmarks.*

## How to use it

1. **Read** `Johnsons-Vibe-Check-grounding.md` end to end — it defines every term used in the case studies.
2. **Pick a case** and run the experimental loop (§IV.4 of the grounding doc). Do the read **blind** — from the artifact's internal choices, ideally *before* you let yourself look at the box office.
3. **Record a falsifiable prediction** (early vs. wrong vs. phantom; will/won't rehabilitate).
4. **Check** against a non-gross settlement signal (rehabilitation trajectory, or the producers' own next-bet vector).
5. **Refine** the framework against what you found, and add your case study.

The framework explicitly invites **challenge**. The cheap move is to confirm the diagonal (well-targeted hits, badly-targeted misses); the real experiment is the **off-diagonal** — and the next open experiment is the **off-*off*-diagonal**: the authentic film that *didn't* endure, which tests whether authentic address is *sufficient* for durability or merely *necessary*.

---

## Status

Early-stage, actively developing. Two off-diagonal cases logged; the theory has survived contact with both and was refined by each. Open questions (the unit-of-measurement problem, the leading-edge predictability question, and whether data can *ever* sample forward) are tracked at the end of the grounding document.

This is a humanities/criticism framework, not a predictive product. It deals in **falsifiable readings**, not guarantees, and it is deliberately neutral on the politics of any given production — by design, as a matter of measurement hygiene.
